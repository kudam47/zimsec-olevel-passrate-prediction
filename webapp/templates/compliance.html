Zimbabwe Cyber and Data Protection Act (2021)

Fully Compliant

This system operates in full compliance with the Zimbabwe Cyber and Data Protection Act of 2021 (Chapter 12:07). The Act regulates the processing of personal data and establishes the Postal and Telecommunications Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe (POTRAZ) as the supervising authority.

The system processes only aggregated district-level data and does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) of individual students, teachers, or community members.

Data Anonymisation Policy

Anonymised at Source
  • All data is aggregated at the district level — no individual records exist
  • Student names, registration numbers, and personal details are never collected
  • Teacher identities and employment details are not stored in the system
  • Geographic data is limited to district and province names — no GPS coordinates or addresses
  • Data cannot be reverse-engineered to identify individuals due to aggregation levels

No Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

Zero PII Storage
Data We Process:
  • District-level pass rate percentages
  • Aggregate pupil-teacher ratios
  • Provincial socioeconomic indicators
  • Resource availability indices
  • Temporal examination statistics
Data We DO NOT Process:
  • Student names or ID numbers
  • Individual examination results
  • Home addresses or contact details
  • Biometric or health data
  • Financial records of individuals

Responsible AI Disclaimer

Ethical AI Framework Applied

This system follows responsible AI principles:

Transparency
All model architectures, parameters, and decision logic are fully documented and open to audit
Interpretability
SHAP values provide transparent explanations for every prediction made by the system
Fairness
Predictions are evaluated for urban/rural bias; scenario tools promote equitable resource allocation
Human Oversight
Model outputs are decision-support tools — all policy decisions require human judgement and review
Accountability
Limitations are clearly documented; the system is not a substitute for educational expertise
Reproducibility
Complete codebase and data pipelines enable full reproduction of all results

Research-Only Usage Notice

Academic Research Only
This system was developed as part of an undergraduate research project and is intended exclusively for academic demonstration, research, and educational purposes. It should not be used as the sole basis for governmental policy decisions without additional validation using official ZIMSEC data and expert review.
